About Me

I’m Noah, a high‑school senior at Lakeside School in Seattle and a competitive freeride skier ranked #485 nationally (U19). Whether I’m dropping into a steep chute or diving into a new dataset, I thrive on challenges that demand adaptability, resilience, and community.

My academic passion centers on finance and entrepreneurship. I’ve completed Wharton’s Business and Financial Modeling specialization, earned research experience under professors from the University of Puget Sound and the University of Sheffield, and pitched winning stock ideas in national competitions.

Off the slopes and spreadsheets, I co‑founded a youth program at my synagogue and volunteer with Friendship Circle, empowering kids with special needs. Wherever I go, I look to build inclusive spaces where everyone can finish their run.
