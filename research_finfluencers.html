Finance Meets #FinTok

Analyzing how social‑media influencers reshape retail investor psychology.

Methodology & Data

I surveyed 216 Gen‑Z investors and scraped 100K finance‑related TikTok/Reddit posts. Statistical tests and topic modeling revealed content trends and correlations with trading behavior.

Headline Insights

  • 70 % of respondents consume finance content on TikTok weekly.
  • 45 % admitted to a FOMO‑driven trade after a viral post.
  • Only 15 % buy immediately; most conduct follow‑up research, hinting at nuanced information use.
