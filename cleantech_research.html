CleanTech & Sustainable Development Goals

How policy levers accelerate startup impact in Indonesia, Brazil, and South Africa.

Research Highlights

This independent paper, completed through the Cambridge Future Scholar Program under Professor Keivan Aghasi, explores how government incentives—grants, tax credits, feed‑in tariffs—enhance the environmental and economic contributions of CleanTech ventures across emerging markets.

  • Analyzed RISE and IEA datasets to benchmark energy‑access & efficiency metrics.
  • Developed a policy‑to‑impact framework linking regulatory rigor with startup growth indicators.
  • Identified Indonesia’s strong grid‑electrification score (71/100) yet weak efficiency incentives (32/100), guiding actionable recommendations.
