Accelerating Sustainability in Emerging Economies

Modeling how CleanTech startups and smart policies can help nations hit the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Project Overview

This independent research, completed through the Cambridge Future Scholar program under Prof. Keivan Aghasi, analyzes how policy incentives foster CleanTech growth in Indonesia, Kenya, and Brazil. I blended literature review, case studies, and a Python simulation to project SDG outcomes.

Key Findings

  • Targeted grants could boost renewable adoption, helping India hit 60% of SDG 7 clean‑energy targets by 2030.
  • Kenya’s solar sector thrives when policy stability pairs with public‑private partnerships.
  • Brazilian regions with incubators + green loans grew clean‑energy jobs 30% faster than peers.
